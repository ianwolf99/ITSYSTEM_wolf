About WolfEye

WolfEye is a powerful system used by IT professionals to conduct reconnaissance on a host and identify the threat landscape. Our system is hosted on the cloud, providing you with the flexibility and scalability you need.

Key Features

  • Advanced host reconnaissance
  • Threat landscape identification
  • Cloud-based system
  • Scalable and flexible
  • Easy to use

How WolfEye Works

  1. Sign up for an account
  2. Access the WolfEye system through the provided URL
  3. Enter the host you want to recon on
  4. WolfEye will analyze the host and provide you with a detailed threat landscape report
  5. Stay ahead of cyber threats and protect your systems
